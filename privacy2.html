Privacy Policy for TablePasteCheck
TablePasteCheck 隱私權政策

Effective Date / 生效日期： November 2025

1. Overview / 概要

English: TablePasteCheck is a local Windows desktop utility designed to help users verify and check pasted data in spreadsheet-like tables. It is distributed as part of the Phoenix Spaces Tools Suite.

中文： TablePasteCheck 是一款在 Windows 上執行的本機工具，協助使用者檢查表格貼上資料是否正確。此應用程式屬於 Phoenix Spaces 工具套件 的一部分。

English: The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations occur locally on your device, without connecting to the internet or external servers.

中文： 本應用程式不會收集、儲存或傳送任何個人資料。所有運算皆在使用者的電腦本機執行，無需連線至網際網路或外部伺服器。

English: TablePasteCheck only accesses clipboard content or files that you explicitly choose. It does not access any system folders, registry, or background data.

中文： TablePasteCheck 僅會存取使用者明確選擇的檔案或剪貼簿內容，不會讀取系統資料夾、登錄檔或背景資料。

4. Third-Party Sharing / 第三方資料分享

English: No data is shared with Phoenix Spaces, its developers, or any third parties. The app includes no analytics, telemetry, or advertising services.

中文： 本應用程式不會與 Phoenix Spaces、開發者或任何第三方分享資料。程式中無分析、遙測或廣告服務。

English: The app does not require an internet connection to function. It does not send or receive any data over the network.

中文： 本應用程式的運作不需要網路連線，也不會主動傳送或接收任何網路資料。

English: Because all actions occur locally, your data remains under your complete control. We recommend standard security measures such as OS updates and antivirus software.
